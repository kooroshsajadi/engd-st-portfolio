Self Introduction

Kourosh Sajjadi completed his bachelor's degree in computer engineering with a focus on software engineering at Islamic Azad University of Najafabad, Iran. He then earned his master's degree in computer engineering, specializing in the data science track, from the University of Pavia, Italy.

During his bachelor's studies, he worked as a .NET software developer at Kasra Co. in Iran, providing access control management solutions, and briefly practiced Scrum mastering. While pursuing his master's, Kourosh participated in an Erasmus exchange study program at TU Eindhoven and interned at Kropman company in the Netherlands, where he developed a text mining solution. His master's thesis, titled "Malware Classification Using Deep Learning," contributed to the usage of deep learning in cybersecurity. Most recently, he worked as a research fellow at the University of Padua, Italy, co-developing a retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) system for the Padua courthouse.
